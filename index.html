Who We Are

Brief History

The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) is a renowned professional society in India focused on advancing electronics, telecommunications, and related fields. Established in 1953, IETE promotes education, research, and industry collaboration. Through publications, events, and certifications, it empowers professionals, academics, and students to excel in these domains.
